## 1. Data Limitations

### Coverage
- Data compiled from secondary sources; original collection methods vary
- Coverage may not be exhaustive for all years/countries/variables

### Accuracy
- Relies on accuracy of original source databases
- Some estimates based on interpolation or projection methods
- Different definitions may be used across countries/sources

### Temporal
- Data represents conditions at time of compilation (March 2026)
- More recent data may be available in original sources

## 2. Methodological Limitations

- Compilation methodology introduces potential selection bias
- Variable harmonization across sources may reduce precision
- Missing data handled by retention of original missing values

## 3. Recommended Use

- Suitable for: trend analysis, comparative research, hypothesis generation
- Not suitable for: clinical decisions, high-precision economic modeling without additional validation
- Always consult original sources for critical applications
